Full Stack Developer
--------------------

I am a dynamic individual who embarked on a journey into the world of programming at the age of 15. Starting with the foundational trio of HTML, JavaScript, and CSS, I gradually delved into the intricacies of web development. My curiosity soon led me to discover the versatility of PHP, unlocking the potential to create more dynamic and interactive web applications. Despite initially embarking on a different path by pursuing a vocational training as an industrial mechanic, I came to a realization during the third year that programming was my true passion. Consequently, I made the bold decision to break away from the mechanical realm and transitioned to a new chapter with TMT GmbH & Co. KG, where I embarked on a 3-year journey to become a certified specialist in application development as a Fachinformatiker Anwendungsentwicklung. Since successfully completing my training, I have been thriving as a Full-Stack Developer at TMT GmbH & Co. KG. Over the years, I have expanded my programming repertoire significantly. I am proficient in Golang, Vue.js, and WordPress plugin development. My familiarity with the WordPress environment is robust, and I continually enhance my PHP skills. Additionally, I have delved into the realms of artificial intelligence development and training, acquired proficiency in Python, explored Flutter, and shifted towards using TypeScript over JavaScript in my projects. My journey as a Full-Stack Developer has been marked by continuous learning, adaptation, and a relentless pursuit of excellence in the ever-evolving landscape of technology.
